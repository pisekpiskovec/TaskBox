Simple to-do manager
to organise daily life.

Today's View – An overview of the most important things

The Today's View section serves as a smart daily planner. You can pin your most important tasks for the day or it will automatically show you the ones that are due today. This gives you an instant overview of your priorities so you can focus on what's most important right now!

Web – Access from anywhere

TaskBox is a web app, which means you can access your tasks from anywhere and on any device - whether it's a desktop or tablet. Just open a browser. The app is designed to be hosted on its own server, giving you full control over your data and availability.

Lists – Organize your tasks by topic

In TaskBox, you can sort tasks into neat lists by project or mood. Each task can also have its own notes, subtasks, reminders and due date. This makes it easy to create a system that fits your work style and pace of life.
